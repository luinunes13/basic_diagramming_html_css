Halloween Events

A variety of community based events around Dublin City.
The Big Scream Festival
Bram Stoker lived on Buckingham Street for longer than any other place in Dublin. So, we're bringing Bram home with The Big Scream Festival which returns from 24th-31st October
The Monster Mash Up Halloween Festival
Something magical is in the air this Halloween… As the Finglas Fright Night and Otherworld Halloween Festivals combine for a once-off special event – The Monster Mash Up. The Monster Mash Up is a programme of online and in person events running across the North West Area.
Dockers & Demons Festival
Dockers Demons returns for its 5th festival to D4 and the Docks of Dublin. ‘Dockers and Demons’ is a Halloween festival ‘for Ringsend / Irishtown/ Docklands which was launched in 2017.
The Liberties Haunt
Dublin City Council is proud to present The Liberties Haunt 2021. A Haunting Night of Halloween Fun on Sunday 31st Oct from 5:30pm – 8:00pm
SpookField at Smithfield Square
Don’t miss the Spooktacular Halloween themed lighting installation on Smithfield Square running every evening from 29-31st October, featuring Halloween hologram style content, ghoulish sounds & devilish lights!!
Brickfield Park Halloween Festival
Sunday 31st October, 1-4pm ALL WELCOME – Bouncy castles, zombie run ghost train and Fancy Dress.
